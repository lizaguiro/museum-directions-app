[Visitor]: Hello! Excuse me. I'm a bit lost. Can you tell me how to get to the Robotics Exhibit from here?

[Guide]: Certainly! Welcome to the museum. Since you are standing here by the Entrance, you need to go straight ahead, past the main Information Desk, until you hit the central elevators.

[Visitor]: Okay, I see the elevators. Do I go up?

[Guide]: Exactly! Take the elevator up to the second floor. When you exit, turn right immediately. Walk past the Space Exhibit on your left, and you'll find the Robotics Exhibit right at the end of the hall. You can't miss it!

[Visitor]: Excellent, thank you. And what about the Science Laboratory? I want to attend the 2 PM chemistry demonstration.

[Guide]: Ah, the Laboratory is located on the first floor. Instead of taking the elevator, stay on this floor and turn left right before you reach it. Walk down that corridor. The laboratory will be the second door on your right, directly opposite the restrooms.

[Visitor]: Understood! Left before the elevator, then second right opposite the restrooms. You've been extremely helpful. Have a great day!

[Guide]: It's my absolute pleasure. Enjoy your exploration!
